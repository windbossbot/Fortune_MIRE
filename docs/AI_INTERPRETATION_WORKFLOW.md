# AI Interpretation Workflow

## Goal

- Generate a composite reading with one click.
- Copy a structured prompt into an AI model.
- Let the AI expand the reading while preserving the original symbolic draw.

## Included symbolic layers

- one tarot card
- one direction state
- one trigram situation
- practical score summary
- one closing oracle line

## Prompt design rules

- Always include both raw draw data and an auto-generated draft interpretation.
- Ask the AI to synthesize, not list components separately.
- Keep the AI output reflective and advisory, not absolute or predictive.

## Current web flow

1. Click the main draw button.
2. Review the combined reading.
3. Copy the generated AI prompt.
4. Paste into the AI tool of your choice for deeper interpretation.

## Why this helps

- The card and auxiliary symbols stay visible and traceable.
- The AI receives enough context to write a richer interpretation.
- The output remains grounded in the original draw rather than free-floating text.
